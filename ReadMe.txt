*System requirements
 --------------------------------
 OS: 		DOS, Windows, Unix, Mac.
 Prosessor:	20 mhz or Higher

*Instructions
 --------------------------------
 Press Forward key to accelerate.
 Press Back key to brake.
 Press Space to shoot.
 Press Right or Left key to turn ship.

 Avoid all Asteroids or you die.
